What We Do

Our Key Focus Areas

We work tirelessly to support underprivileged communities through structured and sustainable programs.

Education Support

Providing basic schooling utilities, free tuition programs, and sponsoring fees for underprivileged children to pave a brighter future.

Food Distribution

Fighting hunger through regular food drives in slums, orphanages, and streets to make nutritious meals accessible to everyone.

Hygiene Awareness

Conducting sanitary pad distribution and hygiene workshops for girls and women, breaking taboos surrounding menstrual health.

Women Empowerment

Conducting self-reliance and self-defense bootcamps, skill training programs, and awareness sessions to uplift underprivileged women.

Clothing Donation

Distributing warmth and dignity by organizing drives to donate fresh clothes and warm blankets during winters to families in need.

Animal Welfare

Providing stray animal shelter assistance, food drives, and emergency medical aid to ensure every stray is treated with compassion.

Our Values

Why NayePankh Stand Out

We believe in delivering measurable, transparent, and direct community-centric support.

Registered NGO

Fully compliant non-profit organisation recognized by government authorities with legal certifications.

Transparent Donations

Every single rupee donated is logged and accounted for, with receipts and periodic utilization reports sent directly to you.

Youth-Led Initiative

Fueled by energetic youth who bring innovation, modern skills, and unwavering passion to the humanitarian space.

Community Driven

Programs designed through active feedback from community leaders and target groups to resolve root issues.

Trusted Volunteers

10,000+ vetted and passionate volunteers who undergo screening and program training for high-integrity service.

Sustainable Impact

Moving beyond temporary relief to establish structured programs like continuous student sponsorships and job skill classes.

Impact Stories

Stories of Real Change

Meet the individuals whose lives were transformed through our programs and your generous contributions.

Education

Rahul's Flight to School

The son of a daily wage laborer, Rahul had to drop out in class 5. NayePankh sponsored his tuition and books. Today, he ranks first in class 8.

Kanpur, UP June 2025
Women Hygiene

Breaking Slum Taboos

Through our regular distribution drives and doctor seminars, over 1,200 women in Noida slums have transitioned to clean sanitary products.

Noida, Delhi NCR Feb 2026
Food Security

No Child Sleeps Hungry

Under our 'NayePankh Rasoi', we serve fresh hot lunch to over 250 orphans and street kids every weekend, combating child malnutrition.

Lucknow, UP April 2026
