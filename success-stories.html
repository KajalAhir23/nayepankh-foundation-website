Education

Karan's Journey to Engineering

The son of a brick-kiln laborer in Kanpur, Karan had dropped out of school to help his father. NayePankh coordinators convinced his family, sponsored his board exam school fees, and provided evening tutoring. Karan cleared state tests and is now studying Civil Engineering.

Kanpur, UP Sponsor Funded
Women Hygiene

Riya's Health Restoration

Riya (14) living in Noida slums used to drop out of school for 5 days every month due to painful infections caused by unsafe sanitation practices. After enrolling in NayePankh's monthly hygiene drives, she received sanitary napkin supplies and safety guidelines. Today she has 100% school attendance.

Noida, Delhi NCR Hygiene Program
Animal Compassion

Slum Stray Sterilization Drive

Unchecked stray populations in Kanpur slums led to frequent dog bite cases, causing kids to fear walking to evening tuition centers. Our volunteer team collaborated with city vets, vaccinating and neutering over 120 street dogs. The slum paths are now safe, and children actively feed these friendly strays.

Kakadev, Kanpur Animal Welfare
