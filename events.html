Track Record

Past Events Timeline

A chronological record of NayePankh's community welfare actions and emergency reliefs on the field.

April 2026

Hygiene First Workshop

East Delhi slums

Our volunteer networks successfully reached out to 400+ adolescent slum girls, distributing biodegradable hygiene napkins and hosting health awareness seminars with expert pediatricians.

December 2025

Winter Warmth Blanket Drive

Kanpur & Noida NCR

Vetted volunteers braved freezing winter nights to distribute over 1,500 fresh heavy wool blankets to homeless citizens sleeping on footpaths and bus shelters.

August 2025

Feasts & School Bags Drive

Lucknow Rural Pockets

Celebrating Independence Day with 800+ slum children. Prepared vegetable meals, held basic painting contests, and supplied school backpacks containing writing materials.

October 2024

Clean & Green Noida Drive

Sector-62 Localities

100+ passionate student volunteers held street cleanup campaigns, created wall murals regarding waste management, and distributed basic trash bins to local street vendors.
