Our Actions

Social Impact Programs

We run continuous community programs focused on key human necessities: schooling, hunger, female hygiene, clothing, and animal welfare.

5,000+ Kids Enrolled
Empowerment

Education Program

We sponsor high school tuition fees, supply free study kits (notebooks, bags, writing kits), and run localized bridge schools to bring school dropouts back into formal classrooms.

Key Objectives:

  • Sponsor full-year tuition for meritorious children.
  • Set up evening tuition centers inside slums.
  • Distribute basic books and tools to 10k+ kids annually.

Academic Mentorship: Beyond books, our college-student volunteers conduct weekend tutoring classes. They guide seniors with career advice, build basic digital skills, and host grammar courses to prepare them for jobs.

How to help: You can sponsor a child's complete academic costs for ₹500/month on our donation portal.

100k+ Hot Meals Served
Nutrition

Food Distribution Program

Combatting malnutrition through weekend kitchen campaigns and emergency dry-ration distributions in rural colonies, street dwellings, and small orphanages.

Key Objectives:

  • Run 'NayePankh Rasoi' weekend fresh meal drives.
  • Support old-age shelters with monthly food grains.
  • Ensure Zero-Hunger corners during flood/disaster periods.

Welfare Kitchens: Our meals are prepared under hygienic conditions, packed, and distributed by volunteers directly to families living on streets, ensuring no food waste and addressing immediate hunger needs.

How to help: You can fund a complete weekend food drive for ₹2500, feeding up to 100 individuals.

12,000+ Sanitary Kits distributed
Health

Women Hygiene Program

We eliminate menstrual stigmas by distributing free sanitary napkins to adolescent girls in rural institutions, slums, and hosting gynecologist consulting sessions.

Key Objectives:

  • Distribute monthly biodegradable sanitary kits.
  • Hold workshops on safety and infection preventions.
  • Debunk age-old taboos surrounding menstrual cycles.

Dignity & Health: Over 70% of females in underprivileged sectors use unsafe cloths during cycles, risking severe pelvic infections. Our program provides them a safe, continuous, and free supply of hygiene napkins.

How to help: A donation of ₹1000 supplies sanitary kits to 10 girls for a whole quarter.

25,000+ Warm Clothes Donated
Welfare

Clothing Donation Program

Providing protection and comfort to homeless individuals. We sort, clean, and distribute wearable clothing and heavy blankets, especially before harsh winter spells.

Key Objectives:

  • Organize winter blanket distribution campaigns.
  • Sort donated clothes to match age groups.
  • Ensure kids have fresh uniforms for local schooling.

Dignity and Warmth: We collect gently used and fresh apparel from colleges and corporate partners. Volunteers examine the garments, pack them neatly, and hand them out in roadside slums.

How to help: You can sign up to donate clothes on our contact page, or fund blanket purchases before winters.

10k+ Leaders Groomed
Development

Youth Leadership Program

Empowering young high school and college students to design social campaigns, lead volunteer chapters, and develop project management capabilities.

Key Objectives:

  • Create local volunteer action networks.
  • Conduct social project design classes.
  • Certify leaders with real-world project skills.

Empowering Tomorrow's Leaders: We believe youth are not just passive helpers but active drivers of changes. Volunteers learn public speaking, coordination, tracking metrics, and digital outreach.

How to help: College students can register as student coordinators on our Volunteer page.

3,000+ Strays Fed & Treated
Compassion

Animal Welfare Program

Promoting a kinder world for strays by conducting daily feeding drives, organizing local stray vaccinations, and collaborating with vets to provide medical treatments.

Key Objectives:

  • Feed street dogs and cows clean water and meals.
  • Host vaccination and anti-rabies drives.
  • Coordinate with veterinary clinics for emergency rescues.

Coexisting with Animals: Street dogs and cats are often neglected. Our volunteers distribute water bowls, feed daily meals, and paste reflective collars to reduce stray accidents at night.

How to help: Donate food grains, veterinary medicine, or sponsor street stray reflector collar drives.
