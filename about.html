Who We Are

Giving Wings to Dreams

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-led non-profit organisation dedicated to transforming lives and empowering underprivileged communities across India.

Our History

How NayePankh Began

Founded by a group of passionate youngsters, NayePankh Foundation started as an informal student community focused on helping neighbors in distress. We recognized that while large charities existed, grassroot micro-problems often remained unaddressed.

What started with a single localized meal drive quickly grew. The raw enthusiasm of youth combined with transparent communication resonated with micro-donors, sparking an expansive welfare movement across multiple cities.

5+
Years of Service
2020

COVID-19 Relief Beginning

Initiated food packet distributions and hygiene supply kits to stranded migrants and slum households during the initial nationwide lockdown.

2021

Official Foundation Registration

Registered legally as a non-profit organization under trust acts, laying structural roots and recruiting over 500 college student volunteers.

2023

Multi-City Community Expansion

Launched structured sponsorship programs sponsoring education for underprivileged children and expanded relief works to 5+ major cities.

Present Day

200k+ Lives Touched

Empowered by a network of 10,000+ volunteers, running regular kitchen drives, sanitary supply chains, and structured academic mentorship setups.

Our Mission

To identify underprivileged, vulnerable sections of society and empower them through sustainable education support, clean nutritional meals, basic healthcare awareness, and gender-inclusive programs, enabling them to lead independent and dignified lives.

Our Vision

To establish a society where every child gets quality schooling, no human sleeps hungry, women live with hygienic support and dignity, and the youth lead social reform programs to build self-reliant communities across the nation.

How We Work

Our Core Values

The guiding principles that dictate our decisions, operational systems, and volunteer relations.

Integrity

Maintaining high moral standards in all programs, ensuring actions match promises made to beneficiaries and donors.

Compassion

Upholding deep empathy and respect for the communities we serve, treating every individual with utmost kindness.

Transparency

Pledging open ledger systems, visible utilization of funds, and structured periodic impact audits for high trust.

Empowerment

Fostering self-reliance rather than perpetual dependence, helping children and women unlock their true capabilities.

Service

Selfless dedication to social reform, prioritizing community upliftment above individual gains and institutional profits.

Certifications & Accreditations

NayePankh operates in full compliance with legal and statutory regulations. Here are our registration details.

NGO Registration

Registered under Section 60 of the Indian Trusts Act, 1882. Officially registered as a Public Charitable Trust driving social welfare.

80G Certification

All donations made to NayePankh Foundation qualify for tax deductions under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act (Placeholder/Applicable).

12A Registration

Approved under Section 12A of the Income Tax Act, granting public charitable status and tax-exemption privileges on spent funds.
