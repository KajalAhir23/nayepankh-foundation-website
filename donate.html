Frequently Asked Questions

Important answers regarding tax exemptions, transparency, and fund allocations at NayePankh.

Over 90% of your donation is directed straight to program operations (buying books, cooking fresh slum meals, buying sanitary pads). The remaining 10% supports storage logistics, printing campaign flyers, and server expenses.
Yes, NayePankh Foundation is a registered Public Charitable Trust. Your donations qualify for up to 50% tax deductions under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Receipts are emailed automatically within 24 hours of successful transactions.
We pride ourselves on our transparency values. We publish visual updates, photos, and monthly grain ledger breakdowns on our verified Instagram handle and email quarterly compliance reports directly to sponsors.
Absolutely! You can arrange collections for school copies, writing kits, stationery, or clean clothing by submitting a form on our Contact Page. Our local coordinators will guide the collection drive.
