Be the Change You Want to See

Channel your skills and passion into a youth-led social movement. Volunteer with NayePankh Foundation and support grassroot changes.

Roles Available

How You Can Help

We have diverse departments to fit your professional skills, interests, and academic background.

Social Media

Help us amplify our message. Run digital campaigns, manage online posts, and engage our digital audience.

Teaching

Mentor underprivileged children. Conduct basic math, grammar, and art sessions at evening centers.

Content Writing

Draft newsletters, blogs, success stories, and press releases detailing NayePankh's monthly campaigns.

Event Management

Coordinate field operations, manage Slum Kitchen distribution setups, and guide local campaign logistics.

Fundraising

Design outreach files, research corporate partnerships (CSR), and coordinate crowdfunding drives.

Graphic Design

Design premium digital flyers, social media carousels, and visual collateral for NayePankh activities.

Web Development

Manage and optimize our website, build portal features, and ensure top-tier user experiences for donors.

Rewards

Benefits of Joining Us

Volunteering at NayePankh is an opportunity to learn, network, lead, and leave a lasting social legacy.

Experience Certificate

Receive an official recommendation letter and certificate validating your tasks, hours, and department performance.

Leadership Skills

Take charge of city volunteer campaigns, coordinate field teams, and master task planning and project execution.

Professional Networking

Collaborate with energetic college students, young professionals, and social leaders nationwide, building lasting networks.

Direct Social Impact

Witness the direct benefits of your actions on underprivileged families, building a deep sense of social purpose and gratitude.
